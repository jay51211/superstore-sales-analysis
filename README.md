**Overview**
This project performs an exploratory data analysis (EDA) on a global superstore dataset using Python.
The objective is to analyze sales performance, profitability, discount impact, and shipping efficiency to derive actionable business insights.

The analysis focuses on understanding how discounts, shipping modes, and seasonal trends affect overall sales and profit.

**Tools & Technologies**

Python

Pandas

Matplotlib

Jupyter Notebook

**Business Questions Addressed**

How do discounts impact average sales?

Which discount levels negatively affect revenue?

Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?

Which shipping modes are most profitable?

**Key Visual Insights**

1. Average Sales by Discount
This chart shows how average sales change with different discount levels, helping identify discount thresholds that may reduce profitability.

2. Discount Impact on Sales
This visualization highlights the overall impact of discounting strategies on sales performance.

3. Seasonal Sales Trends
Sales performance across different time periods, revealing seasonal demand patterns.

4. Profit by Shipping Mode
Comparison of profit generated by different shipping modes to identify the most cost-effective logistics options.

**Key Insights & Findings**

Moderate discounts tend to improve sales, but higher discounts reduce profitability

Sales show noticeable seasonal variation, indicating demand cycles

Certain shipping modes consistently generate higher profit margins

Strategic discounting and optimized shipping choices can significantly improve business outcomes

Conclusion
This analysis demonstrates how data-driven insights can support better pricing, promotional, and logistics decisions.
By optimizing discount strategies and focusing on profitable shipping modes, businesses can improve both revenue and profit.
